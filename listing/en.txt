NAME (38 chars, store limit 45):
Flobro - Always on Top Floating Window

SUMMARY (128 chars, limit 132):
Keep any tab always on top in a floating window. Pin videos, docs or dashboards in a chromeless popup, or float above every app.

DESCRIPTION:
Keep any webpage always in sight. Flobro pops any tab, link or video out into a minimal floating window: no tabs, no address bar, no interface. Just the content, always visible while you work.

And Flobro goes one step further than any other extension. With the free Flobro desktop app for Mac and Windows, one click turns that floating window into a true always on top window that stays above every application on your screen, not just your browser.

FLOBRO FEATURES

- Float any tab: click the toolbar icon and your current tab pops out into a chromeless floating window.
- Float any link: right-click a link and open it directly in a floating window.
- Pin videos, live streams, chats, dashboards or docs and keep them in view.
- Distraction-free: the popup shows only the webpage, nothing else.
- Move and resize the floating window to fit your screen and workflow.
- True always on top: forward any page to the free Flobro desktop app and it floats above ALL applications, with zoom (25-500%), 16:9 snap and pin controls in a toolbar that only appears on hover.
- Free and open source (MIT). No account, no ads.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Click the Flobro icon to float your current tab, or right-click any link and choose Flobro.
2. Move and resize the floating popup wherever you like.
3. Want it above every app, not just Chrome? Install the free desktop app from flobro.app and forward the page with one click.

WHO USES FLOBRO

- Developers: keep documentation, build logs or API responses visible while coding.
- Students: watch a lecture or tutorial while taking notes in another app.
- Analysts and traders: monitor live charts, feeds and news without tab switching.
- Writers and researchers: keep sources and references in view while you write.
- Sports and video fans: follow the match or video in a corner of your screen.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I make a Chrome tab always stay on top?
A: Click the Flobro icon to float the tab in a chromeless popup window. For a window that stays on top of every application, forward it to the free Flobro desktop app with one click.

Q: What is the difference between the floating popup and the desktop app?
A: The popup is a minimal Chrome window without interface. Browsers cannot keep a window above other applications, so for true always on top behavior Flobro hands the page over to its desktop app for Mac and Windows.

Q: Is Flobro really free?
A: Yes. Flobro is free and open source under the MIT license. Development is supported by voluntary donations.

Q: What about my privacy?
A: Anonymous usage statistics are limited to hostnames only, never full URLs, and can be switched off in the options. Details at flobro.app/privacy.html.
